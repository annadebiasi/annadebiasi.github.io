Artwork

by Anna De Biasi

I started drawing primarily with pencils at a young age and later transitioned to colored pencils, charcoal, water color, etc. As I enjoy various mediums, I fell in love with painting the most. I appreciate the fluid and liberating act of each stroke that allows one the ability to choose their own scale.
