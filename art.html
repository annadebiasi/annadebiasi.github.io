Artwork

by Anna De Biasi

Since I was young, I always enjoyed being creative. I started playing with pencil drawings and later moved on to colored pencil, charcoal, etc. As I enjoy various mediums, I fell in love with painting the most. I appreciate the fluid and liberating act of each stroke that allows one the ability to choose their own scale.
